# MUXI - Manifesto

### We believe in open-source software and open standards.

Your agents, your infrastructure, your data – no vendor dependency, no surprise deprecations, no rug pulls. The code is open, the roadmap is public, and you're never locked in.

> ### Our creed
> AI infrastructure should be open, not owned by big tech.
>
> We believe AI systems should be:
> - Self-hostable: Run anywhere, owned by you
> - Observable: See what's happening, always
> - Declarative: Version-controlled and reproducible
> - Open: No secrets, no lock-in
>
> If you build it, *you* should control it.

Bottom line: **You can self-host for free, forever.**
